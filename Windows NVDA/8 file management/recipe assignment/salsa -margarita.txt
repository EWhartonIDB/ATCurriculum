This mango or peach salsa recipe was shared by Colleen.

INGREDIENTS:
List of 8 items
• 1/2 cup chopped mild or sweet onion
• 2 cups diced mangoes or peaches
• 1-2 tablespoons minced, seeded serrano or other small chili
• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh Cilantro
• 3 tablespoons tequila or orange juice or orange liqueur
• 1 tablespoons finely grated lime peel
• 2 tablespoons lime juice
• 1/4 ground cumin
list end

PREPARATION:
Mix well and serve warm. Use as a meat accompaniment or as a delicious topping for sweet potatoes.
