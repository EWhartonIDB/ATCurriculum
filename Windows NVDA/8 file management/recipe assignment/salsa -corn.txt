Corn salsa with purple onion, red bell pepper, jalapeno peppers, cilantro and lime juice.

INGREDIENTS:
List of 7 items
• 2 cups frozen corn kernels, thawed
• 1/3 cup chopped purple onion
• 1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
• 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
• 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
• 1 to 2 tablespoons finely chopped jalapeno pepper
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
list end

PREPARATION:
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl. Cover and refrigerate for 2 to 4 hours. Remove from refrigerator about 30 minutes before serving. Serve corn salsa
with grilled meat or poultry.
Makes about 3 cups corn salsa.
