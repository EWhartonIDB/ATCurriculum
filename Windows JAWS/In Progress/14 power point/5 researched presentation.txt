Power Point 2 -- Choose Your Own Presentation

Requirements:
1. Must choose a color scheme different from the default black and white
2. Must contain at least 8 slides
3. At least 5 slides must have notes
4. Must choose a font different from the default Arial
5. Must use at least one table
6. Must use at least one sound or video clip
7. Must contain at least 5 pictures
8. Must use both visual and audio slide transitions
