The Big Apple Pie
  
Source: Member
  
Ingredients:   
7 Cups thinly sliced Granny Smith Apples 
2/3 Cup Sugar 
2 Tablespoons Cornstarch 
1/2 Teaspoon Cinnamon 
1/8 Teaspoon Nutmeg 
1/8 Teaspoon Salt 
3 Tablespoons Margarine or Butter 

2 Unbaked Pie Shells 
  Instructions:   
Peel and slice apples very thin. Mix all ingredients and pour into large unbaked pie shell. Top with another unbaked pie shell. Make several slits in top of shell and sprinkle with mixture of sugar and cinnamon. Dot with margarine. Bake at 400 degrees for 10 minutes then lower temperature to 350 degrees and bake an additional 35 minutes or until golden brown. 
 
Notes:   
This makes a large pie.
