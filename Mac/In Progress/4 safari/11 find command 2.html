Using the Find Command on a Web Page

"Quick Nav" provides another way of navigating web pages.

Turn quick nav on by pressing the left and right arrow keys together. Now, pressing the left arrow key is like pressing VO-left, and pressing the right arrow key is like pressing VO-Right.

Pressing the up and down arrows will move you through different elements on a webpage. You can choose between a number of item types including links, headings and form fields by pressing the up and left keys together or the up and right keys together to move back and forth through the options.
