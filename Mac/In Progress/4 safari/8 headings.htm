Headings are a great way to browse through a large web page, particularly one with many sections. Headings come in different levels. The lower the number, the larger the section. Here are two commands for using headings with VoiceOver:

vo-command-h takes you to the next heading

vo-u can be used to bring up a list of all the headings on the page
