Pumpkin Pie

1 1/2 cups sugar (3/4)
1 tsp. salt (1/2)
2 tsp. cinnamon (1)
1 tsp. ground ginger (1/2)
1/2 ground cloves (1/4)
4 large eggs (2)
1 can punpkin (1/2)
2 cans evaporated milke (1)
uncooked pie shell

mix sugar salt cinn, ginger in cloves in small bowl. beat eggs in large bowl. stir in punpkin and sugar-spice mixture. gradually stirr in evap milk, pour into pie shell
bake 425 for 15 reduce to 350 for 40-50 or until clean knife
