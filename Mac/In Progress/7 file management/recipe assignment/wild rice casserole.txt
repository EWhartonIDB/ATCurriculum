Wild rice casserole

1 pkg. combination rice
1/2 c celery chopped
1/2 C. onion
1 can mushrooms

Brown, onion, celery, musrooms in butter generously

1 can beef broth
2 T. flour

Cook Rice per pkg. instructions
Add to beef broth
add flour
Put all together in buttered casserole.
Bake 1 1/2 to 2 hours
on top croutons & cheese last 15 minutes
