Downloading and unpacking books from Bookshare

1. Go to www.bookshare.org.  Login with your username and password. If you do not yet have one, have your instructor log you in.

2. Search for a book that you would like to read in audio. Choose the link to download in daisy.  Safari will automatically save the files in your Downloads folder.

3. Choose a book to read in braille. Choose the link to download in brf.  

4. Copy the books to the SD card you will get from your instructor.
