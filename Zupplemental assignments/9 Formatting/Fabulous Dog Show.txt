Fabulous Dog Show Lesson 
  
Instructions:  Find the letters "dog" and replace it with "cat" using Notepad. 
  
Objective:  Learning about find and replace command. 
  
There are fifty fabulous dogs signed up to  
compete in the National Purina Dog Show in Chicago. 
These highly skilled dogs come from all over the world. 
They dress to impress as their owners have created wonderful, 
one-of-a-kind dog outfits.  These intelligent dogs 
come to win a year worth of dog treats and dog toys. 
Currently, there are thirty-six more small dogs than large  
dogs signed up to compete.  More dogs are welcome 
to compete in all of the different events.  If you love dogs, 
this is the dog show for you and your dog.
