One, Two, Buckle My Shoe Lesson

Instructions:  Move the following lines around so they are in numerical order by using the cutting and pasting commands.

Objective:  Learning to cut and paste a line of text.


Five, six, pick up sticks;
Three, four, shut the door;
One, two, buckle my shoe;
Seven, eight, lay them straight;
Fifteen, sixteen, maids in the kitchen;
Eleven, twelve, dig and delve;
Nine, ten, a good fat hen;
Seventeen, eighteen, maids are waiting;
Nineteen, twenty, my platter's empty.
Thirteen, fourteen, maids are courting,
