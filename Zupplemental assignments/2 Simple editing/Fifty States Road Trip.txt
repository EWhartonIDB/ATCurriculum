Stephen and Tabitha are a retired married couple who decide to go on their life-long dream road trip across the United States of America. Starting in their hometown of Portland, Maine, they travelled to all 50 states plus Washington D.C. before finishing their trip in Chicago, Illinois. All the states below are written in the order of when they were travelled, but it's your job to put them into alphabetical order.
