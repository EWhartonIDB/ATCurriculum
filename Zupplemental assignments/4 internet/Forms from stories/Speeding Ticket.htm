Speeding Ticket

Hal is waiting in his car at a red light. He is in a big hurry because he needs to be at work in 10 minutes. Finally the light turns green. Hal begins to drive faster.

Now Hal is driving 45 miles per hour. The speed limit on this street is 35.

Hal approaches another intersection. The light is yellow. Hal presses the gas pedal and goes through the intersection at 50 miles per hour. "I made it!" says Hal.

Hal looks in his rearview mirror. He sees a flashing light. "Oh, no," he says. It's a police car. Hal pulls over to the side of the road. The officer gets out of his car and comes up to Hal's window.

"May I see your license and registration?" asks the police officer.

"Of course, Officer," says Hal. "Is there a problem?"

"Yes, there is. You were driving 25 miles an hour over the speed limit. You're getting a speeding ticket."

"I'm sorry," says Hal. "I'll never do that again."

"Good," says the officer. "It isn't safe."

"I agree," says Hal. "It isn't safe to speed when a police officer sees you."

The End.
