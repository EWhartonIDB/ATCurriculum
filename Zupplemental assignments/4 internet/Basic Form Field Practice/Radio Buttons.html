Radio Buttons

Radio buttons are similar to combo boxes in that you can only choose one of several options.

Unlike combo boxes though, all of the radio button choices appear on the screen at once and can be arrowed between without going into forms mode.

A radio button usually appears before the words that say what it is. If the buttons are created correctly, JAWS will read the name of the button on the actual button, and then again when you arrow past the text next to it. Sometimes JAWS will just say Radio Button on the button, and you must arrow past it to see what it is.

Press space to choose a radio button.

If you want to change your choice, just choose another option.

Practice using radio buttons by answering the questions below.

1. What class period is it now? First Second Third Fourth

2. What is your gender? Male Female Other

3. Choose your age group. Under 18. 18-30 31-40 41-50 51-65 65 and older

4. Choose your ethnicity. Caucasian African American Hispanic Native American Pacific Islander Other

5. What is the temperature like outside today? Frigid Cold Chilly Comfortable Warm Hot Like an oven.

6. Which of the following would you most like to eat right now? Pizza Spaghetti Steak Salad Chocolate

7. What if your favorite thing to do? Sleep Eat Hang out Shop Anything Outside None of the above

8. What is your favorite time of day? Morning Afternoon Evening Night

9. What is your favorite sport? Baseball Basketball Football Soccer Hockey None of the above/I do not like sports.
