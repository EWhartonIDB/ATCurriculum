Check Boxes

Check boxes can either be checked or unchecked.

Each box is like its own yes or no question, where checked means yes, and unchecked means no.

If a question has a list of check boxes after it, you can check as many as you want since each one is separate.

If you want to change your answer, you have to go to the box you want to change and either check or uncheck it.
