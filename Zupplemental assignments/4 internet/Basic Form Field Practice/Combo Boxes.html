Combo Boxes

A combo box is a field where you are given a specific list of choices to pick from.

Go into a combo box with space.

Arrow up and down between the choices.

Press escape on the choice you want to leave the combo box and select an option.

If you want to change your choice simply go back into the box and pick a new one.

Only one choice from a combo box can be chosen at a time.
