Edit boxes

Edit boxes allow you to type whatever you want in them.

You must go into the box before you can type.

Some edit boxes are very large, while others may only have a small amount of space; if you fill the box, anything you type after that will not be written down.

Your commands for reading and editing word documents work in edit boxes.

Press escape to get out of an edit box.
